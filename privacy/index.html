Privacy Policy

Information we collect

There are three categories of information we collect:
Information you provide
Information we can get when you use our services
Information we collect from third parties

Information you provide

When you interact with our services, we collect the information that you choose to share with us.
Registration. When you register through the app, you provide us with a verified phone number. During the process of registering, we will send you an SMS to verify your phone number.
Nickname. You will provide a nickname which will be used and displayed to other users. You can change it anytime in the Edit Profile section of the app.
Date of birth. You will provide your date of birth which will be used and displayed to other users. To encourage correct age representation you cannot change your date of birth after onboarding.
Gender. You will decide which gender between male and female represents you the best for the usage of the services. To encourage correct gender representation you cannot change your gender after onboarding.
Profile pictures. You will provide at least one profile picture during onboarding. You can change it anytime in the Edit Profile section of the app.
Location. SOL is a location based service and in order for the app to fully function we need permission to collect your precise location when you use the app, your location is used to find other users within your selected distance range who will be introduced to you in the Discover section of the app. We determine your location using methods that include GPS, wireless networks, cell towers, WiFi access points.
Music data. You will be asked to authorize SOL to connect to the third-party application Spotify and access your Spotify account, which will enable SOL to build and save your music profile based on the data Spotify provides on your listening habits. Your music profile will be displayed to other users as part of the Discover feature. Other users will be able to see and listen to your music habits (i.e. top songs, artists, genres). SOL may decide to delete or alter your created place at anytime, thus enabling a smooth evolution of the app database and deleting any inappropriate content.
Communications. You also provide us with any other information you send through our services, such as messages.

Information we can get when you use our services

When you use our services, we collect information about how you use them, here are some examples:
Usage information. We collect information about your activity through our service, for example: how you communicate with your matches on SOL, such as time and date of your communications, as well as the users you like or dislike in the Discover feature.

How we use information

Our main goal is to offer the best possible service so you can meet musically minded people in a safe environment. Specifically, we may use your information to:
- develop, operate, improve, deliver, maintain, and protect our services.
- create statistical studies and monitor and analyze trends (all studies and analysis are run on an anonymized basis).
- enhance the safety and security of our products and services.
- send you push notification. If you don't want to receive push notifications, just go to your settings to turn them off.
- verify your identity and prevent fraud or other unauthorized or illegal activity.
- enforce our Terms of Service and other usage policies.
